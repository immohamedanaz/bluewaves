Kandy Cultural Tour

The cultural capital, home to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic.

Tour Itinerary

Immerse yourself in Kandyan heritage and natural splendor

  • 08:00 AM

    Hotel Pickup

    Enjoy a comfortable morning ride to the beautiful hill capital of Kandy.

  • 09:30 AM

    Royal Botanical Gardens

    Wander through the massive and stunning Peradeniya Royal Botanical Gardens, famous for its incredible orchid collection.

  • 11:30 AM

    Kandy Lake Walk

    Take a serene stroll around the picturesque Kandy Lake (Bogambara Lake) located right in the heart of the city.

  • 12:30 PM

    Authentic Sri Lankan Lunch

    Indulge in an authentic rice and curry banquet at a local restaurant overlooking the hills.

  • 02:00 PM

    Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

    Visit the revered Sri Dalada Maligawa, housing the sacred tooth relic of Lord Buddha. Experience the spiritual atmosphere and intricate architecture.

  • 04:00 PM

    Cultural Dance Show

    Watch a captivating Kandyan cultural performance featuring traditional drumming, vibrant costumes, and fire-walking.

  • 06:00 PM

    Return to Hotel

    Depart from Kandy as our driver softly brings you back to your accommodation.
