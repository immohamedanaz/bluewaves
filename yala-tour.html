Yala Safari Adventure

Wildlife sanctuary famous for leopards, elephants, and coastal beauty.

Tour Itinerary

Thrilling wildlife encounters in Sri Lanka's premier national park

  • 05:00 AM

    Early Morning Pickup

    Start before dawn to reach the park right as the gates open and wildlife is most active.

  • 06:00 AM

    Enter Yala National Park

    Board a rugged 4x4 Jeep and begin exploring Yala's diverse ecosystems, aiming to spot the elusive Sri Lankan Leopard.

  • 09:30 AM

    Breakfast by the Beach

    Enjoy a packed breakfast at Patanangala beach inside the national park, an untouched stretch where the jungle meets the ocean.

  • 10:30 AM

    Follow the Elephant Herds

    Continue tracking wildlife. Witness herds of elephants, sloth bears, crocodiles, and an incredible array of bird species.

  • 12:30 PM

    Exit Park & Lunch

    Wrap up the safari adventure and head to a local hotel/restaurant in Tissamaharama for a hearty lunch.

  • 02:00 PM

    Return Journey

    Travel back to your hotel offering time to rest after an exciting morning.
