Tour Itinerary

An amazing day over the Indian Ocean's giant marine life and golden beaches

  • 06:00 AM

    Harbor Transfer

    An early pickup to transfer you straight to the Mirissa Fishery Harbour where our premium boat awaits.

  • 06:30 AM

    Whale Watching Cruise

    Set sail into the deep sea to spot Blue Whales, Sperm Whales, and playful pods of Dolphins in their natural habitat.

  • 11:30 AM

    Return to Shore

    Dock back at Mirissa Harbor after an awe-inspiring marine adventure on the Indian Ocean.

  • 12:30 PM

    Seafood Lunch on the Beach

    Enjoy freshly caught seafood and local dishes at a vibrant beachfront restaurant in Mirissa.

  • 02:00 PM

    Coconut Tree Hill

    Take a scenic walk up to Mirissa's famous Coconut Tree Hill, an iconic tropical viewpoint perfect for photos.

  • 04:00 PM

    Return Journey

    Board the taxi for a relaxing transfer back to your accommodation to rest after an early start.
