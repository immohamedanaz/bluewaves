Ella Nature Tour

Tour Itinerary

Discover Sri Lanka's most scenic mountain landscapes

  • 07:00 AM

    Scenic Mountain Drive

    Depart your hotel and enjoy a phenomenal drive up the winding roads through misty tea plantations toward Ella.

  • 09:00 AM

    Hike to Little Adam's Peak

    Take an easy but rewarding hike to the summit of Little Adam's Peak for panoramic views of the cascading mountain ranges.

  • 11:30 AM

    Nine Arch Bridge

    Walk to the iconic Nine Arch Bridge, an architectural marvel set amidst dense jungle and tea fields. We'll wait to catch sight of a passing train.

  • 01:00 PM

    Lunch at Ella Town

    Relax and enjoy a flavorful Sri Lankan lunch at a cozy cafe in the laid-back town of Ella.

  • 02:30 PM

    Ravana Falls Viewpoint

    Stop by the striking Ravana Waterfalls, cascading from an oval-shaped concave rock outcrop, linked to ancient legends.

  • 04:00 PM

    Return Journey

    Begin the comfortable drive back to your hotel as dusk settles over the central highlands.
