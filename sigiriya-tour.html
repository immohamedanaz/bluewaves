Sigiriya Rock Fortress Tour

Explore the ancient rock fortress and palace ruin, offering breathtaking views.

Tour Itinerary

Experience the majestic history and natural beauty of Sigiriya

  • 07:00 AM

    Hotel Pickup

    Begin your journey with an early morning pickup in our comfortable luxury vehicles.

  • 09:00 AM

    Arrival at Sigiriya

    Arrive at the base of the majestic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, an ancient palace and fortress complex that is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

  • 09:15 AM

    Climb the Lion Rock

    Ascend the 1,200 steps past the Mirror Wall and the famous Sigiriya Frescoes to reach the summit for breathtaking panoramic views.

  • 11:30 AM

    Explore the Water Gardens

    Descend and explore one of the oldest landscaped gardens in the world, marveling at the ancient engineering.

  • 12:30 PM

    Authentic Sri Lankan Lunch

    Enjoy a delicious, traditional Sri Lankan lunch featuring fresh local ingredients at a nearby highly-rated restaurant.

  • 02:00 PM

    Dambulla Cave Temple

    Visit the impressive Dambulla Cave Temple complex, famous for its ancient Buddhist murals and hundreds of statues.

  • 04:00 PM

    Return to Hotel

    Conclude your royal exploration as our driver safely transports you back to your hotel.
