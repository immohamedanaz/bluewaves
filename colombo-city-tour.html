Tour Itinerary

A carefully curated journey through Colombo's best landmarks

  • 08:30 AM

    Premium Hotel Pickup

    Start your day with a comfortable pickup in our luxury vehicles. Your professional guide will provide a brief overview of the day's adventure.

  • 09:00 AM

    Viharamahadevi Park Stroll

    Begin with a refreshing morning walk through Colombo's largest and most lush urban park, setting a tranquil tone for the day.

  • 09:45 AM

    National Museum of Colombo

    Dive deep into Sri Lanka's rich heritage exploring ancient artifacts, royal relics, and beautiful colonial architecture.

  • 11:00 AM

    Independence Square & Arcade

    Visit the iconic site that commemorates Sri Lanka's independence. Marvel at the intricate stone carvings and peaceful surroundings.

  • 11:45 AM

    Gangaramaya Temple

    Experience spiritual serenity at one of Colombo's most important temples, known for its eclectic mix of modern and traditional architecture.

  • 12:45 PM

    Authentic Sri Lankan Lunch

    Enjoy a curated culinary experience featuring the best of local spices and flavors at a premium Colombo restaurant.

  • 02:00 PM

    Colombo Lotus Tower

    Take an elevator ride to the top of South Asia's tallest tower for breathtaking 360-degree panoramic views of the city skyline.

  • 03:15 PM

    Pettah Market & Red Mosque

    Immerse yourself in the bustling energy of Pettah's streets and photograph the visually stunning Jami Ul-Alfar (Red Mosque).

  • 04:15 PM

    Galle Face Green Sunset

    End the tour perfectly by strolling along the ocean promenade, viewing the Old Parliament, and watching a beautiful Indian Ocean sunset.

  • 05:30 PM

    Hotel Drop-off

    Return comfortably to your hotel after an unforgettable day exploring the capital.
