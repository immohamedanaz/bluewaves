Galle Heritage Tour

Historic Dutch Fort with charming streets and stunning ocean views.

Tour Itinerary

Coastal charm mixed with colonial history

  • 08:30 AM

    Coastal Drive Transfer

    Enjoy a scenic southern coastal drive towards Galle in our premium vehicle.

  • 10:00 AM

    Explore Galle Dutch Fort

    Wander through the beautifully preserved 17th-century fortification, filled with Dutch colonial villas, boutiques, and art galleries.

  • 11:30 AM

    Fort Walls & Lighthouse

    Walk across the ramparts overlooking the Indian Ocean, culminating at the iconic white Galle Lighthouse.

  • 12:30 PM

    Lunch at a Heritage Cafe

    Dine at one of the stylish heritage cafes located within the cobblestone streets of the fort.

  • 02:00 PM

    Maritime & National Museum

    Discover the rich history of southern Sri Lanka, tracing its trading history back several centuries.

  • 03:30 PM

    Unawatuna Beach

    Take a short drive to the nearby palm-fringed Unawatuna Beach to relax on golden sands before the journey back.

  • 05:00 PM

    Return Journey

    Head back to your hotel, reflecting on the historical charm of Sri Lanka's southern capital.
